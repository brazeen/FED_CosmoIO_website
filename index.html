About

Cosmo's Story

Meet Cosmo, the astronaut on a bit of a pickle. Stranded on the moon with his trusty spaceship, he's got a small problem - there isn't enough fuel to zoom back to Earth. But here's the twist: his spaceship runs on knowledge, and that's where you jump in! Get ready for an adventure where your brainpower can save the day. Whether it's math, music, or just general smarts, your answers will be like rocket fuel for Cosmo's spaceship. Cosmo isn't one to back down. He's faced with the vastness of space, the moon around him, and Earth in the distance. But with your help, he's confident he can make it back home. So, buckle up for a trip through questions that cover all sorts of cool stuff. Every right answer nudges Cosmo's spaceship closer to Earth. It's a team effort to get Cosmo back home safe and sound. Are you up for the challenge?

The Developers

This trivia game was developed by Brandon Koh, Toh Yang Yi and Donovan Lye from The School of Infocomm Technology, Ngee Ann Polytechnic, using three.js, bootstrap, and jquery libraries, as well as assets from Sketchfab and LottieFiles. OpenTrivia Database was used to generate trivia questions, and RestDB was used to store the leaderboard values. Thank you for playing our game!

This is a gamified website.

The website uses three.js for the 3D menu, Bootstrap for the responsiveness and a trivia API for the questions to be asked. Lottie animations are also used in the game.
